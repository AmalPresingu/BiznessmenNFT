Script

In an alternative timeline, Earth has become corrupt. It all started in 1948 when an evil mastermind named Orson Voltaire genetically engineered an ape. He inserted human brain genes in the ape. The ape killed Voltaire and escaped from the laboratory. A century later, a variety of bioengineered humanoids have blended into society. They act and look just like humans, until they're compromised. They seek to destroy humanity and take over the planet. Men in black and blue are tasked with taking down all humanoids.

They go by the name of... BIZNESSMEN.
