LORE

In an alternate timeline, Earth has become corrupt. It all started in 1948 when an evil mastermind named Orson Voltaire genetically engineered an ape. The ape killed Voltaire and escaped from the laboratory. A century later, a variety of bioengineered humanoids have blended into society. They act and look just like humans, until they're compromised. They seek to destroy humanity and take over the planet by slipping into the common workforce. Men in black and blue are tasked with infiltrating these businesses and ensuring our safety. After beating the humanoids at their own game, they receive $BIZ as a reward.

They go by the name of... BIZNESSMEN.
